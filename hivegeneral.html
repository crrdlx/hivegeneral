Connect in the Hive General community chat

  1. Subscribe to the Hive General community.
  2. Go to https://chat.peakd.com/t/hive-116655/0.
  3. Login via Hive Keychain, click "Hive General", then "General".
Note that Sting requires Hive Keychain. On desktop, the Hive Keychain browser extension is required. On mobile, the Hive Keychain app is required. Sting can then be accessed on mobile from the app's built-in browser
